Features Of Altro Smart

Elevate your home to its smartest level.

Altro Smart Model X is truly unique in design as it combines the connectivity of a smart lock with the convenience of a video doorbell into a single smart device.

Made for Security. Made for Convenience.

With features like keyless entry, 2-way audio, 1-way video, custom passcodes, 24×7 activity logs, and more, Altro Smart Model X is the smart choice for the next generation smart home owner.

Your front door, controlled from your smartphone.

Real time video alerts over WiFi & Bluetooth gives you the convenience to watch who’s at your front door, talk to them from anywhere in the world, and even grant/deny entry access; all on your smartphone.

Shared Home? Share Access!

The Altro Smart Model X along with the Altro Smart mobile app gives you the ability to create custom passcodes for friends & family. You can also set expiration dates to these codes to align with the time for a guest to leave.

Voice Control & Pairing

The Altro Smart Model X can pair with your existing home control devices like an Alexa or a Google Home. Use your voice to command your Altro Smart Lock to lock/unlock or notify you about your smart home.

Stay Connected. Stay Updated.

The Altro Smart Model X connects to your home WiFi and sends you alerts with a recorded 10 seconds video every time someone is at your doorstep. Our smart lock also maintains a 30 day log of all door activity on the Altro Cloud, which can be purchased with a subscription.
